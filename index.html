About Me

I’ve always wanted my work as a people photographer to serve a greater good, that is, to fulfill the need we all have as humans for meaning and inspiration in our lives. And because so much of the meaning in our lives comes from our relationships, I’ve focused my abilities almost exclusively on creating artistic photography that highlights and reaffirms the meaning we derive from our most treasured relationships.
